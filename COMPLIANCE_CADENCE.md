# Legal, Safety & Compliance Cadence (AIM + regulated healthcare)

## Scope
Applies to: website copy, SEO pages, ads, social posts, chat scripts, intake flows, and any promotional offers presented alongside regulated services.

## Weekly (15–30 min)
- Website scan for high-risk language drift:
  - Superlatives/comparatives: best, leading, world-class, top
  - Specialization language: specialist, specializes in
  - Guarantees: pain-free, immediate results, no risks/side effects
  - Inducements: discounts, prizes, giveaways, time-limited pricing
  - Quantified claims: % cure rates, recovery timelines
- Review new content drafts BEFORE publish.

## Monthly (45–90 min)
- Privacy/security check:
  - Intake pathways: where PHI is collected and stored
  - AI assistant boundaries: no diagnosis/medical advice; no PHI to LLM
  - Access review: WordPress roles, Vercel env vars, audit logs
- Platform policy check:
  - Meta/Google policy warnings, disapprovals, or account health

## Pre-launch gate (mandatory)
Before any new landing page/promo/ad goes live:
- Compliance read-through vs CPTA advertising/marketing expectations
- Confirm claims are truthful/verifiable; remove or cite anything quantified
- Ensure offers/promotions are not presented as inducements for regulated services

## Escalation triggers (same-day)
- Any regulator complaint, takedown notice, or ad account restriction
- Any page containing: “specialist”, “guaranteed”, “no risk/side effects”, or cure-rate claims

## Logging
- Keep a simple change log: URL → what changed → why → approver → date
