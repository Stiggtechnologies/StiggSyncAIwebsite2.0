## ⚠️ RISKS & CONSIDERATIONS

### Risk 1: Learning Phase Disruption
**Issue:** Major restructure triggers Google's learning phase (14-21 days)  
**Mitigation:** Implement in phases, keep existing campaigns running during transition  
**Impact:** Temporary 15-20% dip in performance during learning

### Risk 2: Budget Dilution
**Issue:** Splitting into 3 campaigns might spread budget too thin  
**Mitigation:** Start with current $75/day, increase to $100/day if performance warrants  
**Impact:** Each campaign needs $20-30/day minimum for statistical significance

### Risk 3: Keyword Cannibalization
**Issue:** Keywords might overlap between campaigns  
**Mitigation:** Use exact match for service-specific, phrase match for general  
**Impact:** Minimal if structured correctly
