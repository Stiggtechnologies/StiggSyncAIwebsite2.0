## 📊 A/B Testing Framework

### Phase 1: Ad Creative Testing (Week 1-2)

**Variable:** Ad creative design and messaging

**Control Group (A):**
- Ad 1: Professional Treatment (ad-1-professional-treatment.jpg)
- Budget: $12/day
- Audience: Physiotherapy - Edmonton North

**Test Variations (B, C, D, E):**
- Ad 2: WCB Care (ad-2-wcb-care.jpg) - Budget: $8/day
- Ad 3: Recovery Success (ad-3-recovery-success.jpg) - Budget: $12/day
- Ad 4: MVA Focus (ad-4-mva-focus.jpg) - Budget: $10/day
- Ad 5: Team Trust (ad-5-team-trust.jpg) - Retargeting only

**Metrics to Track:**
- Impressions
- CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- CPC (Cost Per Click)
- CPM (Cost Per 1000 Impressions)
- Relevance Score

**Success Criteria:**
- CTR >1.5% = Winner
- CPC <$3.00 = Winner
- Relevance Score >7/10 = Winner

**Decision Point (Day 7):**
- Pause ads with CTR <0.5%
- Double budget on ads with CTR >2.5%
- Keep testing ads with CTR 1.0-2.5%

### Phase 2: Ad Copy Testing (Week 2-3)

**Variable:** Primary text and headlines

**For Each Winning Creative, Test 3 Copy Variations:**

**Example for Ad 1 (Professional Treatment):**

**Variation A (Pain Point Focus):**
- Headline: "Struggling with chronic pain?"
- Text: "Don't let pain control your life. Book a FREE assessment today and start your recovery journey."
- CTA: "Book Now"

**Variation B (Benefit Focus):**
- Headline: "Get Back to Life Pain-Free"
- Text: "Professional physiotherapy that works. ✅ Same-day appointments ✅ Direct WCB billing ✅ Expert therapists"
- CTA: "Call Now"

**Variation C (Authority Focus):**
- Headline: "Edmonton's Most Trusted Injury Clinic"
- Text: "38+ years serving Edmonton. 5-star rated. Expert team ready to help you recover faster."
- CTA: "Learn More"

**Metrics to Track:**
- CTR (from Phase 1)
- Landing page click-through rate
- Form submission rate
- Call conversion rate

**Success Criteria:**
- Form submission rate >5%
- Call rate >2%

### Phase 3: Landing Page Testing (Week 3-4)

**Variable:** Landing page design and content

**Control Landing Page (A):**
- Current aimphysiotherapy.ca homepage
- General physiotherapy messaging
- Contact form + phone number

**Test Variations:**

**Variation B (Service-Specific Pages):**
- /services/physiotherapy/ - Physiotherapy-focused
- /services/mva-physiotherapy/ - MVA-specific
- /services/wcb-rehabilitation/ - WCB-specific
- Match ad to specific landing page

**Variation C (High-Converting Template):**
- Hero section with video testimonial
- Before/after patient stories
- Prominent "Book Free Assessment" CTA
- Social proof (5-star reviews)
- FAQ section
- Multiple CTAs throughout

**Elements to Test:**
1. Hero headline
2. CTA button color (green vs. blue vs. orange)
3. CTA button text ("Book Now" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Free Assessment")
4. Form length (3 fields vs. 5 fields vs. 7 fields)
5. Phone prominence (top vs. bottom vs. sticky header)
6. Trust signals (badges, reviews, certifications)
7. Video vs. static image

**Metrics to Track:**
- Bounce rate
- Time on page
- Scroll depth
- Form submission rate
- Phone click rate
- Form abandonment rate

**Success Criteria:**
- Bounce rate <40%
- Form conversion >8%
- Combined conversion (form + call) >12%

---

### Phase 4: Call-to-Action Testing (Week 3-4)

**Variable:** Type of conversion action

**Control (A): Contact Form**
- Standard web form
- Name, email, phone, service needed, message
- Immediate confirmation email

**Variation B: Direct Call**
- Prominent "Call Now" button
- Click-to-call on mobile
- Track call duration and outcome

**Variation C: Booking Widget**
- Integrated scheduling tool
- Choose time/therapist
- Instant confirmation
- Requires implementation: Calendly or similar

**Variation D: SMS Option**
- "Text us at 780-250-8188"
- Quick response promise
- Track SMS→booking rate

**Metrics to Track:**
- Form submissions per day
- Call volume per day
- Booking completion rate
- Show-up rate (actual patients)

**Success Criteria:**
- Combined daily conversions >15
- Show-up rate >75%

## 🔬 Testing Methodology

### Statistical Significance
- Minimum 100 clicks per variation before making decisions
- Minimum 7 days of data before declaring winner
- 95% confidence level required

### Budget Allocation
- Equal budget split during testing phase
- Winner gets 70% of budget after test concludes
- Losers get 15% each (for monitoring)

### Decision Framework
```
IF CTR < 0.5% AND 7 days passed → PAUSE
IF CTR > 2.5% AND 100+ clicks → SCALE (increase budget 50%)
IF CPL > $75 AND 20+ leads → PAUSE
IF CPL < $30 AND 20+ leads → SCALE (increase budget 100%)
```

## 🚨 Red Flags & Kill Switches

**Pause Ad If:**
- CTR <0.3% after 3 days
- CPL >$100 after 10 leads
- Relevance Score <4/10
- Zero conversions after $150 spent

**Pause Campaign If:**
- Total CPL >$75 after 30 leads
- ROI <200% after 30 days
- Patient show-up rate <60%
- Ad spend >5% of AIM revenue
