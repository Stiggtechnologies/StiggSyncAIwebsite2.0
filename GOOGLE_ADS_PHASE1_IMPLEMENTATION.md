## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Problem: New keywords not getting impressions
**Cause:** Bids too low or low quality score  
**Fix:** Increase max CPC by $1-2, wait 48 hours

### Problem: CTR drops significantly
**Cause:** New ads underperforming  
**Fix:** Pause specific low-CTR ads, keep winners

### Problem: CPC spikes above $5
**Cause:** Too aggressive bidding on exact match  
**Fix:** Lower exact match bids by $0.50-1.00

### Problem: Conversions don't increase
**Cause:** May need more time (7 days minimum)  
**Fix:** Wait 14 days before judging, landing page may need work
