## AI Agents Feature Breakdown

### 🔍 **Monitoring Agents** (What They Watch)

| Agent | What It Monitors | Why It Matters | Typical Failure Detected | Customer Value |
|-------|------------------|----------------|-------------------------|----------------|
| **Vibration Analyst** | Motor/bearing vibration patterns | 70% of rotating equipment failures show vibration signatures first | Bearing degradation, misalignment, imbalance | Prevents catastrophic motor failures ($10K-$100K each) |
| **Temperature Monitor** | Operating temperatures, heat patterns, cooling system performance | Overheating = #1 cause of electrical failures | Overheating motors, failing cooling, electrical hotspots | Prevents fires, electrical failures, product spoilage |
| **Pressure Tracker** | Hydraulic/pneumatic pressure, pump performance, leak detection | Pressure anomalies = leaks, pump wear, system inefficiency | Pump wear, compressed air leaks, hydraulic failures | Reduces energy waste + prevents system failures |
| **Energy Analyst** | Power consumption patterns, efficiency trends, load analysis | Energy inefficiency = maintenance need (and wasted $) | Degrading compressors, worn belts, motor issues | Saves $2K-$10K/month in power costs |
