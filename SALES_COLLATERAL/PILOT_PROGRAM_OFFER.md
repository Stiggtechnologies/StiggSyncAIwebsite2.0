## Pilot Process: Step-by-Step

### Before Pilot Starts (Pre-Work)

**Week -1: Pilot Agreement**
- Sign 1-page pilot agreement
- Define asset list (5-10 assets)
- Set success criteria
- Schedule kickoff

**Documents Needed:**
- [ ] Equipment list (make, model, age, maintenance history)
- [ ] Sensor inventory (what sensors exist on each asset)
- [ ] Network access details (for remote connectivity)
- [ ] Maintenance logs (last 12 months of failures - optional but helpful)

---

### Week 1: Kickoff & Connection

**Kickoff Meeting (2 hours)**
- Introductions (your team + our Customer Success Manager)
- Review pilot goals and success criteria
- Technical walkthrough (how we'll connect to sensors)
- Equipment tour (virtual or on-site)
- Q&A

**Who Should Attend:**
- ✅ Project sponsor (Operations Director, Plant Manager)
- ✅ Maintenance manager/supervisor (daily user)
- ✅ IT contact (if remote access needed)
- ⚠️ Optional: CFO/owner (if they want to see firsthand)

**What We Do (Days 2-7):**
- Connect to existing sensors remotely
- Install monitoring agents (software-only, no equipment downtime)
- Configure AI models for your asset types
- Set up user accounts and dashboards
- Initial training (1-hour session)

**Your Involvement:**
- Provide network/sensor access credentials
- Attend training session
- Test dashboard/mobile app

---

### Weeks 2-4: Learning Phase

**What's Happening:**
- AI agents monitor equipment 24/7
- Establish "normal" operating baselines
- Calibrate anomaly detection thresholds
- Begin pattern recognition

**What You'll See:**
- Equipment health scores appearing (green/yellow/red)
- Historical data analysis (if provided)
- First anomaly detections (but not yet predictions)

**Weekly Check-In (15 minutes):**
- Review baseline progress
- Answer questions
- Adjust alert thresholds if needed

**Your Involvement:**
- ~30 minutes per week (check dashboard, attend call)
- Provide context on equipment behavior if needed
- Report any equipment issues that occur

---

### Weeks 5-8: Prediction Phase

**What's Happening:**
- First failure predictions generated
- Alerts sent (mobile + email + dashboard)
- Validation begins (did predictions match reality?)
- ROI tracking activated

**What You'll See:**
Example alert:
> **⚠️ PREDICTION ALERT**  
> Asset: Mixer Motor #3  
> Issue: Bearing degradation detected  
> Confidence: 89%  
> Estimated failure: 9-12 days  
> Recommended action: Schedule bearing replacement during next planned downtime (Saturday 2/28)  
> Parts needed: SKF 6310 bearing (link to supplier)  
> Labor: 3-4 hours  
> Impact if not addressed: $85K downtime + $12K emergency repair  

**How to Validate:**
1. **Schedule recommended maintenance** (during downtime)
2. **Inspect the asset** (was our prediction correct?)
3. **Document findings** (photo, inspection report)
4. **Track outcome** (failure prevented? False alarm? How much saved?)

**Weekly Check-In (30 minutes):**
- Review predictions from past week
- Discuss validation results
- Calculate ROI for prevented failures
- Adjust prediction sensitivity if needed

**Your Involvement:**
- ~2 hours per week (respond to alerts, schedule maintenance, validate predictions)
- Critical: Document outcomes (we'll provide template)

---

### Week 8: Pilot Debrief & Decision

**Debrief Meeting (1 hour)**

We'll review:
1. **Predictions Made:** How many alerts over 60 days?
2. **Accuracy Rate:** What % of predictions were correct?
3. **Failures Prevented:** How many major issues avoided?
4. **ROI Achieved:** Total savings vs pilot cost
5. **Team Adoption:** How many people using it daily?
6. **Lessons Learned:** What worked? What didn't?

**Decision Time:**

**Option 1: Convert to Annual Contract** ⭐ Best Value
- ✅ 10% discount on Year 1 (saves $3,000-$6,000)
- ✅ Lock in current pricing (protect against future increases)
- ✅ Expand to more assets (add 10-50 more assets)
- ✅ Priority support + quarterly business reviews
- ✅ Onboarding credit applied (pilot cost credited toward annual)

**Option 2: Continue Month-to-Month**
- ✅ Flexibility (cancel with 30 days notice)
- ✅ Same features as annual
- ❌ No discount (full price: $799-$2,499/month)
- ❌ No price lock (subject to increases)

**Option 3: Exit Pilot**
- ✅ 30-day notice to cancel
- ✅ Full data export (keep your insights)
- ❌ Lose 50% pilot discount
- ❌ Lose early adopter benefits

**Our Recommendation:** If pilot proved value (prevented even 1 failure), go annual. ROI justifies commitment.

### Q: What if we don't have 60 days before we need a decision?

**A: We can do an accelerated 30-day pilot, but accuracy suffers.**

- **Ideal:** 60 days (30 days learning + 30 days predicting)
- **Minimum:** 30 days (15 days learning + 15 days predicting)

**Tradeoff:** Shorter pilot = less prediction accuracy = harder to validate ROI.

**Recommendation:** If you're in a rush, start the pilot ASAP and make the decision at 45 days instead of 60.
