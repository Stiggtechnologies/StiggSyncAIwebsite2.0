# SyncAI — Series A Use of Funds (Draft)

**Raise target:** $8.0M (Series A)

## Summary allocation (24-month plan)
1. **Go-to-market (Sales + Marketing): 45% (~$3.6M)**
   - Hire + ramp SDRs/AEs
   - Demand gen, content, events, outbound tooling
   - Build partner motion (OEMs / integrators)

2. **Product & Engineering (R&D): 35% (~$2.8M)**
   - Reliability, integrations (CMMS/ERP/SCADA), enterprise security
   - Vertical-specific models for Food & Beverage
   - Data pipeline + MLOps + alert quality

3. **Customer Success & Implementation: 15% (~$1.2M)**
   - CSMs + solutions engineering
   - Onboarding playbooks, ROI tracking, case studies

4. **G&A / Finance / Legal: 5% (~$0.4M)**
   - Finance ops, audit readiness, security/compliance, legal

## 24-month milestones investors should underwrite
- **$1M–$2M ARR** with repeatable sales motion
- CAC payback < 12 months; LTV:CAC > 5x
- NRR > 110% with measurable expansion
- 3–5 public case studies with quantified ROI
- Platform integrations + SOC2 plan (or equivalent)
