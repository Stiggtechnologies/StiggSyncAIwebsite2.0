## 1. Title
- SyncAI — AI-powered predictive maintenance for mid-market Food & Beverage
- Raising: **$8M Series A** (target) | Use: scale GTM + deepen product

## 2. The Problem (Acute + Quantified)
- Mid-market plants run **critical assets with thin margins**
- **Unplanned downtime costs $15K–$50K/hr** + waste + late deliveries
- Maintenance teams are stuck in **reactive firefighting**

## 3. Why Now
- Sensor/PLC data availability is high; AI tooling is mature
- Manufacturers are forced to modernize due to labor shortages + compliance
- Mid-market underserved: enterprise suites too heavy; CMMS too shallow

## 4. Solution
- SyncAI monitors equipment data and uses **specialist AI agents** to predict failures
- Outputs: alerts, recommended actions, parts planning, compliance documentation
- Deploys fast; software-first (no proprietary hardware required)

## 5. Product (How it works)
- Connect → Learn baseline → Predict/Recommend → Document savings
- Typical time to value: **~45 days** (first prevented failure)
- Integrations: CMMS / ERP / SCADA (roadmap)

## 6. ROI Proof (Customer economics)
- Base case (representative): **$30K/yr** subscription
- Annual quantified benefit: **$3M–$9M** (primarily reduced downtime)
- Payback: **days to weeks** (per ROI calculator)
