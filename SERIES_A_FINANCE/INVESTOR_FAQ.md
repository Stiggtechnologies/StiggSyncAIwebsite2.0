# SyncAI — Investor FAQ (Draft)

## Company
**Q: What does SyncAI do?**
A: SyncAI is a predictive maintenance platform for mid-market industrial operators (starting in Food & Beverage) that turns equipment data into failure predictions and recommended actions via specialist AI agents.

**Q: Who is the ideal customer?**
A: Mid-market Food & Beverage manufacturers (100–500 employees) with $5M–$50M of production assets and meaningful unplanned downtime costs.

## Problem / ROI
**Q: How do you quantify value?**
A: Primarily through reduced unplanned downtime (often $15K–$50K/hr), plus labor efficiency, parts cost reduction, and compliance/admin time savings. The ROI calculator shows conservative/base/aggressive cases.

**Q: What’s the typical payback?**
A: In many scenarios payback is within weeks; the illustrative model shows days when downtime costs are high.

## Product
**Q: Do customers need new sensors?**
A: Not necessarily. SyncAI is software-first and can use existing sensors/PLC/SCADA data; additional low-cost sensors can be recommended when gaps exist.

**Q: What’s the moat?**
A: Workflow integration + vertical-specific models + accumulating labeled operational data + playbooks that reduce time-to-value.

## Go-To-Market
**Q: What’s the sales motion?**
A: Outbound to target accounts → demo → 60-day pilot → annual subscription → expansion to more assets/sites.

**Q: Who signs?**
A: Economic buyer is typically CFO/COO; champions are Plant Manager/Maintenance leadership.

## Economics
**Q: What’s pricing?**
A: Core tier typically $2,499–$4,999/month for mid-market; enterprise/custom for multi-site.

**Q: Gross margin?**
A: Target ~82% at scale.

## Fundraise
**Q: How much are you raising and for what?**
A: Target $8M Series A to build a scalable GTM engine, deepen F&B product, and expand integrations and CS.

**Q: Key 24-month milestones?**
A: $1–$2M ARR with repeatable CAC + sales cycle, NRR >110%, multiple ROI case studies, and completed integration roadmap.
