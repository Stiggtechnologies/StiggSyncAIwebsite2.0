# Demo Script (30 minutes) + Discovery — Food & Beverage

## Goal
Turn 1st meeting into a **pilot commitment** and a scheduled next step.

## Pre-call prep (5 min)
- Identify likely critical assets: packaging line motors/bearings, refrigeration compressors, pumps
- Pull 1 trigger: hiring, expansion, compliance messaging
- Prepare a 1-slide ROI snapshot (use `ROI_CALCULATOR.csv` inputs with conservative assumptions)

## Agenda (send in invite)
1) Current downtime + maintenance reality (8 min)
2) What SyncAI does + how it works (7 min)
3) Walkthrough: predictions, alerts, ROI tracking (10 min)
4) Pilot design + success criteria (5 min)

## Discovery questions (pick 6–8)
**Downtime & throughput**
- What’s your best estimate of **unplanned downtime %**?
- When downtime hits, what’s the main cost driver: lost production, waste, overtime, penalties?
- What’s your downtime cost/hour range?

**Assets & failure modes**
- Which assets are the “usual suspects” (top 5–10)?
- Any recent incidents you never want to repeat?
- Do you run multi-shift? Any seasonal peaks?

**Process & systems**
- Do you use a CMMS/ERP today? What’s working/not working?
- Do you have sensors/PLC/SCADA data available?

**Buying & timeline**
- Who besides you cares most about this: maintenance lead, CFO, quality?
- If we could prove value in 60 days, what would the approval path look like?

## Demo narrative (talk track)
- “CMMS tells you **what you planned**. SyncAI tells you **what’s likely to fail**.”
- Show: asset health overview → a specific prediction (“Bearing failure in 12 days, 92% confidence”) → recommended action → ROI tracker.
- Emphasize: fast setup, no data science required, measurable savings.

## Pilot ask (close)
> Based on what you shared, I recommend a 60-day pilot on 5–10 critical assets (packaging/refrigeration + one pump/motor cluster). If we agree on success criteria and hit them, we roll into an annual.

**Lock next step:** schedule pilot design workshop (45 min) with Ops + Maintenance this week.

## After-call email
- Recap pains + numbers
- Pilot scope + owners
- Meeting link for pilot design workshop
- Attach one-pager
