# SyncAI 60-Day Pilot Offer (Design Partner / Early Customer)

## Who this is for
Food & Beverage manufacturers in ON/QC with measurable unplanned downtime and 5–10 critical assets to monitor.

## Pilot scope
- **Duration:** 60 days
- **Assets:** 5–10 (packaging line assets + refrigeration/compressors + pumps/motors)
- **Data:** connect to existing sensors/PLC where available (history optional)
- **Users:** Ops + Maintenance + (optional) Quality

## What SyncAI delivers
- Asset health dashboard + alerts
- 7–30 day failure predictions where signals exist
- Recommended actions + parts/maintenance suggestions
- Weekly check-ins; end-of-pilot ROI summary

## Success criteria (agree in writing)
Pick 3–5:
1) Predictions validated against observed anomalies
2) Lead time: average warning >= 7 days
3) Prevented incident(s) or avoided downtime hours documented
4) Reduction in emergency work orders/overtime
5) ROI documentation suitable for CFO approval

## Commercials
- Pilot priced at **50% of annualized plan** (creditable toward annual if converted)
- Annual subscription begins after pilot when success criteria met

## Conversion plan
End-of-pilot meeting includes:
- ROI recap (downtime savings + labor/parts impact)
- Rollout scope (assets + facilities)
- Annual agreement + implementation timeline

## What we need from the customer
- Named Ops owner + Maintenance owner
- Access to sensor/PLC data where available
- Agreement to participate in ROI tracking

## Optional: case study
If the pilot delivers measurable outcomes, customer agrees to a lightweight case study (quote + metrics; can be anonymized).
