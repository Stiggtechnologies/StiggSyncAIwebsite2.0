## Decision criteria (must be explicit)
- What does “pilot success” mean?
- What is the financial threshold (ROI/payback)?
- Who signs and by when?

## Risks & mitigations
- Data availability → confirm early; start with assets that already have sensors
- Change management → weekly touchpoints; quick wins
- Finance skepticism → ROI tracking from day 1
