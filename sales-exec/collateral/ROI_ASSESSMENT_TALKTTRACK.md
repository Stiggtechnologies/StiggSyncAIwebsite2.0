# ROI Assessment Talk Track (15–20 min)

## Objective
Get to a quantified “this is worth a pilot” conclusion using the customer’s numbers.

## Inputs to ask for
- Downtime % (unplanned)
- Cost of downtime per hour (range is OK)
- # of major incidents/year (or hours of downtime/year)
- Maintenance budget and staff size

## Script
1) **Confirm the pain**
> If you could wave a wand and fix one operations problem this quarter, would it be downtime, labor, quality, or something else?

2) **Anchor on downtime cost**
> When the line stops, is it mainly lost production, waste/spoilage, overtime, or late shipment penalties?

3) **Do the math live** (use ROI_CALCULATOR)
- Conservative case: 20% downtime reduction
- Base case: 35%

4) **Translate to a decision**
> If the conservative case pays back in under a quarter, does it make sense to validate via a 60-day pilot?

## Key soundbites
- “One prevented failure often pays for a year.”
- “We’re not replacing your CMMS; we’re adding predictive intelligence.”
- “Pilot first, then scale.”
