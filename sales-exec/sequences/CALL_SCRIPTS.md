# Call Scripts (Ops + Maintenance) + Voicemails

## Call opener (Ops leader: Plant Manager / Director Ops)
**Goal:** Permission-based conversation + book ROI call.

> Hi {{FirstName}}, it’s {{Name}} with SyncAI. I know you weren’t expecting my call — do you have 30 seconds?

If yes:
> We work with ON/QC food manufacturers to reduce unplanned downtime on packaging/refrigeration and the ‘usual suspect’ assets. Quick question: is downtime a top 3 focus this quarter?

If yes/“sometimes”:
> Makes sense. When downtime hits, what’s usually the biggest driver — line stoppage, spoilage, overtime, or late shipments?

Pivot to meeting:
> If I could run a quick ROI sanity check with your numbers (15 minutes), would next Tue/Wed work?

## Objection handling
**“We already have a CMMS.”**
> Perfect — we don’t replace CMMS. CMMS schedules work; we predict failures early so you can *choose* the best time to do the work.

**“No budget.”**
> Understood. The reason I called is the math is usually lopsided: preventing one incident often pays for a year. Would you be open to validating the ROI first?

**“Send info.”**
> Happy to. What would make it relevant — packaging line reliability, refrigeration, or reducing emergency call-outs? And who else should be on it (maintenance manager)?
