# SyncAI Outbound Cadence (90-day)

## Target audience (per account)
- **Primary:** Plant Manager / Director of Operations
- **Co-buyer/Champion:** Maintenance Manager / Reliability / Engineering
- **Economic buyer:** CFO/Owner (brought in after ROI is quantified)

## Cadence principles
- Multi-thread early (Ops + Maintenance).
- Tight personalization: **1 custom sentence** (equipment/process, plant, or trigger).
- Every touch has a micro-CTA: **“Worth a 15-min ROI sanity check?”**

## Standard cadence (14 business days)
**Day 1:** Email #1 + LinkedIn view/follow
**Day 2:** LinkedIn connect + short note
**Day 3:** Call #1 (Ops) + voicemail
**Day 4:** Email #2 (problem proof + 1 metric)
**Day 6:** Call #2 (Maintenance) + voicemail
**Day 7:** LinkedIn message #2 (after connect)
**Day 9:** Email #3 (ROI snapshot + pilot offer)
**Day 11:** Call #3 (Ops) + “breakup” voicemail
**Day 14:** Email #4 (breakup) + LinkedIn message #3

Then recycle Tier A into a lighter **intent watch + monthly nurture**.

## Daily activity targets (one founder/AE + one SDR)
- New accounts enrolled/day: **10–15 (Tier A/B mix)**
- New contacts added/day: **25–35**
- Emails sent/day: **40–60** (highly targeted, not blasts)
- Calls/day: **20–30**
- LinkedIn actions/day: **30–50** (views, connects, messages)

## KPI targets (leading indicators)
- Connect acceptance: **25–40%**
- Email open rate: **45–65%**
- Reply rate: **6–12%**
- Positive reply rate: **2–5%**
- Meetings booked: **3–6 per 100 accounts enrolled**

## Hand-off points
- **Positive reply → Meeting booked within 48h**
- **Meeting held → ROI assessment + next step scheduled same week**
- **Pilot proposal → Mutual Action Plan created + dated**
