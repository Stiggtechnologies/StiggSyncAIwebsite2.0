# Opportunity Playbook — From First Touch to Close

## 1) First touch → reply
- Personalize with 1 sentence: equipment, plant, or trigger event.
- CTA is small: **15-min ROI sanity check**.

## 2) Reply → Meeting
- Book within 48 hours.
- Ask to include maintenance leader if ops lead is the first responder.

## 3) Meeting held → Qualified
Exit criteria:
- downtime cost/hour or bounded range
- top 5 critical assets identified
- champion identified
- next step scheduled

## 4) ROI assessment → Pilot proposed
- Use conservative scenario (20% reduction) to avoid credibility risk.
- Share pilot scope and success criteria.

## 5) Pilot active → Close
- Weekly: what alerts happened, what actions taken, what was avoided.
- Track a running ROI log.

## 6) Close → Expansion plan
- Identify next asset cluster + second facility.
- Schedule QBR cadence.

## Email templates (internal)
- Meeting recap
- Pilot kickoff
- Pilot weekly update
- ROI summary + annual proposal
