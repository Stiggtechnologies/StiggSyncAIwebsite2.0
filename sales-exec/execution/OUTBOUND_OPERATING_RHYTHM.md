# Outbound Operating Rhythm (Simple)

## Daily
- 60 minutes prospecting/enrichment (Tier A)
- 60 minutes sending targeted emails
- 60 minutes calls (Ops + Maintenance)
- 30 minutes LinkedIn
- 30 minutes CRM hygiene (next steps + dates)

## Weekly
- Monday: pipeline + stuck deals
- Wednesday: outbound performance review (copy, channels)
- Friday: pilot review + ROI proof capture

## Monthly
- Refresh ICP segmentation (which F&B subsegments convert best)
- Update talk tracks and objections
