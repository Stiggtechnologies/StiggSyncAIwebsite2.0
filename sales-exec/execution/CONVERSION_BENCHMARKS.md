# Conversion Benchmarks (Targets)

These are targets for ON/QC mid-market F&B with tight lists.

## Top of funnel
- Connect acceptance: **25–40%**
- Email reply rate: **6–12%**
- Positive reply rate: **2–5%**
- Meeting booked per 100 accounts enrolled: **3–6**

## Mid funnel
- Meeting hold rate: **70–85%**
- Qualified rate (held → qualified): **35–55%**
- Pilot start rate (qualified → pilot started): **40–70%**

## Bottom funnel
- Pilot → closed-won: **40–60%**
- Qualified opp → closed-won: **30–35%**

## Cycle time targets
- Reply → meeting: < 7 days
- Meeting → pilot start: 14–21 days
- Pilot start → close: 30–45 days
