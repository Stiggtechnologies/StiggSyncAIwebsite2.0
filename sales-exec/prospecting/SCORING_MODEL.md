# SyncAI Outbound Scoring Model — Food & Beverage (ON/QC)

## Goal
Prioritize **accounts most likely to buy in 45–75 days** and produce **$12K–$50K ACV** with a fast pilot.

## Account scoring (0–100)
Use this to assign Tier **A (80–100)** / **B (60–79)** / **C (<60)**.

### A) Firmographic fit (0–30)
- Employees 100–1,000 (ideal 150–500): **+15**
- Ontario/Quebec plant (GTA/Hamilton/KW; Montreal/Laval): **+10**
- Food & Beverage manufacturing (dairy, bakery, beverages, meat, packaged foods): **+5**

### B) Asset intensity + downtime exposure (0–30)
- Multi-shift / 24-7 ops: **+10**
- Packaging lines OR refrigeration/compressors are core: **+10**
- 20+ critical assets / multiple lines: **+10**

### C) Trigger events (0–20)
Any 1–2 triggers usually push an account to Tier A.
- Hiring: maintenance manager/ops director/reliability roles: **+8**
- Expansion: new line/plant/capex news: **+6**
- Quality/compliance focus (HACCP/CFIA audit language): **+3**
- Downtime hints in reviews/news (delays, recalls, shortages): **+3**

### D) Tech readiness (0–10)
- Uses CMMS/ERP or mentions “OEE/SCADA/PLC”: **+6**
- Has existing sensors/automation vendor ecosystem: **+4**

### E) Contactability + buyer access (0–10)
- Ops leader identified (Plant Manager/Director Ops) + maintenance leader: **+6**
- CFO/owner identified + reachable: **+4**

## Disqualifiers (subtract)
- Contract manufacturer with very low margins and no capex: **-10**
- Very small (<75 employees) or single small plant: **-15**
- No maintenance team / fully outsourced: **-10**

## Persona fit scoring (0–5)
For each contact.
- Plant Manager / Director Ops / VP Ops: **5**
- Maintenance Manager / Reliability Eng: **4**
- CFO / Controller: **3** (economic buyer; needs ROI framing)
- IT Manager: **2** (influencer)
- HR / Procurement only: **1**

## Segmentation for messaging
- **Packaged foods + packaging lines** → focus on unplanned downtime + on-time delivery + overtime
- **Dairy / cold chain** → focus on refrigeration reliability + spoilage prevention
- **Meat processing** → compliance + sanitation windows + unplanned downtime cost
- **Beverages** → high-throughput lines + seasonal peaks

## Output
Every account must have:
- 1 Ops leader + 1 Maintenance leader + (optional) Finance buyer
- Tier + priority score
- A short “pain hypothesis” (1 sentence) used in first-touch personalization
