## 📥 HOW TO DOWNLOAD FROM UNSPLASH

1. Go to link above
2. Find an image you like
3. Click the image
4. Click **Download** (green button, top right)
5. Image downloads automatically (high-res, free)
6. No attribution required (but appreciated)

---

## 📥 HOW TO DOWNLOAD FROM PEXELS

1. Go to link above
2. Find an image you like
3. Click the image
4. Click **Download** (green button)
5. Select size: **Large (1920×?)** or **Original**
6. Image downloads automatically
7. Free for commercial use

## 🎨 QUICK CANVA WORKFLOW

1. Go to **Canva.com** (free account works)
2. Click **Create a design** → **Custom size** → 1200 × 628 px
3. Upload your downloaded stock photos
4. Upload `aim-logo.jpg` from the creative folder
5. Add text elements:
   - **Headline** (large, bold, white text with black shadow)
   - **Phone number** (very large, easy to read)
   - **AIM logo** (bottom right corner)
6. Export as **JPG** (high quality)
7. Repeat for all 5 ad creatives
