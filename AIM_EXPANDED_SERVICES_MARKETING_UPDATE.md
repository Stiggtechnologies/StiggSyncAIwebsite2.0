## Strategic Rationale

### Why This Expansion Matters:

**1. Chiropractor Addition:**
- **Market Overlap:** 40-50% of injury patients seek chiro + physio combined care
- **WCB/MVA Opportunity:** Chiropractors approved for WCB claims (parallel revenue stream)
- **Higher Visit Frequency:** Chiro patients average 8-12 visits (vs 6-10 physio)
- **Competition:** Moderate (less saturated than physio in Edmonton)

**2. Pelvic Health Addition:**
- **Underserved Market:** Only 5-10 pelvic health specialists in Edmonton (HIGH demand, LOW supply)
- **High LTV:** Pelvic floor therapy = 10-20 sessions typical (vs 6-12 general physio)
- **Lower CPC:** Pelvic health keywords = $2-4 CPC (vs $4-6 physio)
- **Demographics:** Women 25-45 (postpartum), Men 45-70 (post-surgery)
- **Referral Network:** OB/GYNs, urologists (strategic partnerships)

### New Target Audiences:

- **Search Behavior:** "chiropractor near me," "WCB chiropractor," "sports chiropractor"

- **Search Behavior:** "postpartum physiotherapy," "pelvic floor therapy," "incontinence treatment"

### Why AIM Wins in Pelvic Health:
