## 🎓 Sales Methodology

### The SyncAI Sales Process

**Step 1: Outbound Prospecting** (SDR)
- Target: Plant managers, operations directors (Ontario/Quebec F&B)
- Channel: LinkedIn, email, phone
- Hook: "Free ROI assessment" (use calculator)
- Goal: Book 30-min demo

**Step 2: Discovery Demo** (AE)
- Understand: Pain points, downtime costs, budget
- Demo: Show dashboard, predictions, ROI tracking
- Calculate: Their specific ROI using calculator
- Goal: Progress to pilot conversation

**Step 3: Pilot Proposal** (AE)
- Offer: 60 days, 5-10 assets, 50% discount ($1,250/mo)
- Frame: "Prove the value before you commit"
- Terms: Success guarantee (prevent $10K or refund)
- Goal: Signed pilot agreement

**Step 4: Pilot Execution** (CS + AE)
- Week 1: Onboard, connect sensors, train
- Weeks 2-4: AI learns, baselines established
- Weeks 5-8: Predictions, validation, ROI tracking
- Goal: Prevent 1-2 failures, document ROI

**Step 5: Conversion** (AE)
- Present: Pilot results, ROI achieved
- Offer: Annual contract (10% discount), expand assets
- Alternatives: Month-to-month or exit (if unsuccessful)
- Goal: Annual contract signed, expansion path defined

**Step 6: Expansion** (CS)
- Quarterly: Business reviews, ROI reporting
- Identify: More assets, additional facilities, new use cases
- Upsell: Add 10-20 assets per year (+15-20% ACV)
- Goal: 110%+ net revenue retention
