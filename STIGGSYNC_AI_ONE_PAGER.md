# StiggSync AI
## Industrial AI Infrastructure for Alberta Operations

---

### **What We Do**

StiggSync AI is an **industrial AI infrastructure platform** that powers autonomous operations for asset-intensive industries. We deploy 15 specialized AI agents that integrate into existing industrial environments, augmenting maintenance, reliability, engineering, and executive decision-making systems.

**Think AWS IoT for industrial operations — purpose-built for Alberta.**
